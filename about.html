Who are we?

ACIN, company in the market for almost 20 years, started its activity in 1999 as a training center in IT. However, entrepreneurship and vision of its directors allowed to quickly win another dynamic, focused on the hardware and software area. Since then, ACIN values for continuous focus on innovation and uses cutting edge means in the area of Cloud Computing, leading many market segments.

The entire software development process is guided by rigor, commitment and experience of our team, allowing present simple and intuitive solutions that meet the needs of our customers, ensuring their loyalty. This business strategy has kept a steady growth over the years, which translates into strengthening and distinction in the market of information technologies in Portugal and abroad.
