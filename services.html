iGest - Billing Software

iGEST is an online billing and management platform, certified by the Tax Authority under number 1480 / AT. Allows issue invoices and waybills from a smartphone, with or without internet connection, computer or POS terminal.

iDOK - Document Management Software

iDOK is a document management software, which allows fast document routing, dematerialized internal information flows and optimizes the operation and organization of the bodies. With IDOK, the documentation shall be preserved digitally.

iMed - Clinical Management Software

IMED is the simplest and most complete online clinic management software used in Portugal. With this integrated solution, it offers all the tools necessary for quick and efficient service, essential for space management in healthcare.
